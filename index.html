Our Story


One grey rainy day in Seattle, Pat came up with a recipie for a new cookie. Something to highlight a Seattle favorite: Salmon!

You might be put off by the fact that cookies should not taste like fish... but fear not. It's a delight and you should try one! It's the perfect pairing with a cup of coffee.

Stop by one of our stores and taste your first salmon cookie. We are sure you won't be disappointed.
